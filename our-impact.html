Our Impact Together

Thank You for Being There When It Matters™

GreaterGood.org celebrated our 12 year anniversary in 2019!

Together, our impact spans the world, from renovating animal shelters and feeding hungry homeless pets in the United States, to building schools and funding medical centers in impoverished countries, to protecting some of the most critical habitat for endangered species around the globe. None of it is possible without you. Join us as we celebrate 12+ years of being There When it Matters™.

View our yearly financial information here to see exactly how your donations have helped us further our mission.

To date, GreaterGood.org has given over $250 million in cash and in-kind grants.

A Timeline of our Accomplishments Together

Because of your dedication, together we have done so much

Your dedication to help, your passion for a variety of causes, your daily actions, showcase you understand that when our whole community comes together, and everyone takes action, we can accomplish big things. Here’s just some of what we’ve achieved thanks to donors like you…

We have distributed over 3,500,000 books to children in need.

We have distributed over 250,000,000 meals to hungry rescue animals.

We have protected over 31,000 acres of critical jaguar habitat in partnership with seven ranchers.

We have provided over 300,000 safe bus rides for girls get to and from school.

We have renovated over 120 animal shelters, giving over 190,000 shelter pets happier, healthier lives while they wait for their forever families.

We have recorded 25,523 biodiversity records through Madrean Discovery Expeditions, which have been featured in scientific journals 8 times.

We have provided 477,647,030 liters of clean water to schools in Laos, South Sudan, & Sierra Leone.

We have raised nearly $1,000,000 with FreeKibble Flights to Freedom to fund Wings of Rescue to transport thousands of adoptable pets to shelters where they can find loving, forever homes.

We have given over $13,000,000 to disaster relief.

We have funded the education of over 300 girls in developing countries.

We have funded over 150,000 vaccinations for pets in need.

...and MUCH more!
